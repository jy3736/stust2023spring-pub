課程簡介 - 數位系統設計實務 (2023 Spring)

課程說明

「數位系統設計實務」是一門以可程式化邏輯閘陣列 (FPGA) 為平台的數位系統設計課程，將涵蓋以下主題：

  1. 基礎邏輯元件：了解數位系統的基礎構建邏輯閘(Logic gates)，以及它們在 FPGA 中的實現。
  1. 組合邏輯(Combinaltional logics)：設計和實現具有組合邏輯的數位系統，包括使用多工器 (mux)、編碼器、和解碼器。
  1. 算術運算元件(Computational components)：設計和實現算數元件，例如加法器、減法器和乘法器。
  1. 記憶體元件(Memory components)：了解和使用鎖存器、翻轉邏輯和寄存器在數位系統設計中。
  1. 序向邏輯(Sequential logics)和有限狀態機 (FSM)：使用 FPGA 設計和實現序向邏輯系統。
  1. 期末微型專題(Final project)：課程將以微型專題作為課程學習總結，學生將實現一個 4 位元資料寬度的小型串列外設介面 (SPI) 元件。

課程將提供實際操作、設計、及測試體驗，讓學生利用 FPGA 學習數位系統設計的實用技能。

Course Description

"Practical Digital Systems Design” is a course that covers the fundamentals of digital systems design, with a focus on the use of Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) as a platform for implementation. The course will cover the following topics:

  1. Basic Logic Gates: Understanding the basic building blocks of digital systems and their implementation in FPGA.
  1. Combinational logics: Design and implementation of digital systems with combinational logic, including the use of multiplexers (mux), encoders, and decoders.
  1. Computational components: Design and implementation of arithmetic components such as adders, subtractors, and multipliers.
  1. Memory components: Understanding and using latches, flip-flops, and registers in digital systems design.
  1. Sequential logics and Finite State Machines (FSMs): Design and implementation of sequential systems with FPGAs.
  1. Final project: The course will conclude with a final project in which students will implement a tiny Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI) component with 4-bit data width.

The course will offer hands-on experience with industry-standard design tools, enabling students to acquire practical skills in digital systems design and testing using FPGAs.
